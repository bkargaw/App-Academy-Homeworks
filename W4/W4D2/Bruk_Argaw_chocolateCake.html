Ingredients

  1. 2 2/3 cups all-purpose flour
  2. 3/4 cup plus 1 tablespoon granulated sugar
  3. 1/3 cup light brown sugar
  4. 1/4 cup best-quality cocoa powder
  5. 2 teaspoons baking powder
  6. 1 teaspoon baking soda
  7. 1/2 teaspoon salt
  8. 3 eggs
  9. 1/2 cup plus 2 tablespoons sour cream
  10. 1 tablespoon vanilla extract
  11. 3/4 cup unsalted butter, melted and cooled
  12. 1/2 cup corn oil
  13. 1 1/3 cups chilled water

Directions
  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease and flour two 9 inch round pans. Sift flour, baking soda and salt together 3 times and set aside. In a small saucepan, heat water, chocolate and 1/2 cup sugar, stirring constantly, for 2 minutes. Remove from heat and cool to lukewarm.
  2. Cream butter and sugar until light and fluffy. Add 3 eggs, one at a time, beating thoroughly after each egg. Add half of flour mixture and beat until smooth.
